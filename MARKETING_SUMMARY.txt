COMMON QUESTIONS & ANSWERS (FOR PITCHING)
================================================================================

Q: Why is decentralization better than centralized alternatives?
A: No key custody (users control tokens), transparent on-chain logic,
   no vendor lock-in, network effects from permissionless executors,
   censorship-resistant.

Q: How do you prevent bad executors?
A: On-chain reputation system, public history, paid only on success,
   smart contract verification of conditions, economic incentives.

Q: What's your competitive advantage?
A: Polkadot native (50+ parachains access), adapter-first (infinite
   protocol support), true decentralization (vs Gelato, Alchemy),
   30-70% cheaper than centralized services.

Q: When is mainnet?
A: Q2 2025 (post-audit). Currently on testnet for validation.

Q: How do you acquire users?
A: Community evangelism, protocol partnerships, DeFi word-of-mouth,
   referral incentives, content marketing, YouTube tutorials.

Q: What's the path to profitability?
A: 1-3% platform fees on execution rewards. Gross margin 40-50%.
   Break-even at ~500K users, profitable at 1M+ users.

Q: How do you scale to multiple chains?
A: Deploy contracts to each chain, XCM for Polkadot parachains,
   bridge integrations for other chains (Ethereum, Polygon, etc).

Q: What about regulatory risk?
A: Positioned as infrastructure (not securities), no custody of user
   funds (decentralized), full transparency (on-chain), proactive legal.
